# Usage

These styles are provided by the `tropical-components` package on npm.

### Headers

```html
<h1>Hello, World!</h1>
<h2>Hello, World!</h2>
<h3>Hello, World!</h3>
<h4>Hello, World!</h4>
<h5>Hello, World!</h5>
<h6>Hello, World!</h6>
```

### Titles

```html
<h1 class="title">Hello, World!</h1>
<h2 class="title">Hello, World!</h2>
<h3 class="title">Hello, World!</h3>
<h4 class="title">Hello, World!</h4>
<h5 class="title">Hello, World!</h5>
<h6 class="title">Hello, World!</h6>
```

### Paragraph & Quote

```html
<p>
    I will be the leader of a company that ends up being
    worth billions of dollars, because I got the
    answers. I understand culture. I am the nucleus. I
    think that’s a responsibility that I have, to push
    possibilities, to show people, this is the level
    that things could be at.
</p>

<blockquote>
    <p>
        I will be the leader of a company that ends up
        being worth billions of dollars, because I got
        the answers. I understand culture. I am the
        nucleus. I think that’s a responsibility that I
        have, to push possibilities, to show people,
        this is the level that things could be at.
    </p>
    <small>Kanye West, Musician</small>
</blockquote>
```

### Colors

```html
<p class="text-muted">Hello, World!</p>
<p class="text-primary">Hello, World!</p>
<p class="text-info">Hello, World!</p>
<p class="text-success">Hello, World!</p>
<p class="text-warning">Hello, World!</p>
<p class="text-danger">Hello, World!</p>
```
